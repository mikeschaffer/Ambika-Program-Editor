AMBIKA PROGRAM EDITOR
Created in 2026 by Mike Schaffer. Send any notes and bug reports to mike@schaffer.com
OVERVIEW
This is a full front-panel editor and patch librarian for the Mutable Instruments Ambika 6-voice hybrid synthesizer. Every parameter on each part — oscillators, filter, envelopes, LFOs, and the mod matrix — can be edited here and sent live to the synth over MIDI, and full programs and banks can be saved to or loaded from disk in the Ambika's native SD card .PRO file format.
CONNECTING & LOADING BANKS
Select your Ambika's MIDI IN and OUT ports and channel in the top right of the window — the status dot will turn green once connected. To browse a bank of patches, click LOAD... in the BANK section, or click A to load a factory bank. Click any slot in the bank view to load that program into the editor.
CHANGING PATCHES
Use the ◀ ▶ arrows in the PROGRAM section to step through patches, or click the program name to jump directly to a slot. Editing any knob, switch, or matrix slot updates the synth in real time over MIDI.
SAVING & REQUESTING
REQUEST FROM SYNTH pulls the currently selected program on the Ambika into the editor. SAVE... in the PROGRAM section writes the current patch to a .PRO file on disk. SAVE... in the BANK section saves the whole bank, or use EXPORT FOR SD CARD in the bank view to package it for the Ambika's SD card.
